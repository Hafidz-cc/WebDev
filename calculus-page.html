About us

Calculus can feel scary, but it's way easier when you're not doing it alone. We help each other understand limits, derivatives, and integrals without the usual headache. Expect lots of problem-solving, "aha!" moments, and maybe a few bad math jokes.

Group rules!!

1 Be respectful to everyone in the group
2 Come prepared — at least try the problems first
3 No sharing answers without explaining how
4 Let the organizer know if you can't make it
5 Keep distractions to a minimum during sessions
