About us

From cells to ecosystems, we explore the living world together. We make sense of complex processes like cellular respiration, genetics, and evolution through discussions, diagrams, and a lot of curiosity. Biology is best when you learn it with others who are just as fascinated — and just as confused.

Group rules!!

1 Respect all questions — no such thing as too basic
2 Read the assigned chapter before each session
3 Bring your own notes and be ready to share insights
4 No disruptions during group explanations
5 Give advance notice if you cannot attend
